The particles worth knowing

Three rules of thumb

  • Don't translate them — feel them. 啦 laa1 is a friendly push; 喇 laa3 means something just changed.
  • 嘅 ge3 comes before other particles; question particles like 咩 me1 and 呀 aa3 come last.
  • Start with just 呀 aa3, 啦 laa1 and 咩 me1. Even those three make you sound far more natural.
