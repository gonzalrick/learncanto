Six small apps that take you from your very first sound to holding a conversation. Work top to bottom — or jump to whatever you need today.

The path

Foundations 入門

The six tones, the sounds, jyutping, and your first building-block words. Begin here.

Basics 基礎

300+ survival words — greetings, numbers, food, directions, emergencies.

L1.5BUILD

Real-situation dialogues, 300 more words, and the grammar that links them into sentences.

L2SPEAK

Conversational 講廣東話

Sentence-final particles, connectors, and patterns that make you actually sound local.

Daily practice
EARDRILL

Listening Dojo 聽力訓練

Train your ear at adjustable speed: listen & reveal, "what did you hear?", and number drills.

How to use it: tap ▶ anywhere to hear a word, mark things ✓ as they stick, and let your progress save as you go. Audio uses your device's built-in Cantonese voice — phones and Macs tend to do best. New here? Start with Foundations and don't rush the tones.
